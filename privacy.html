Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2026

SaltShore Systems (“we,” “our,” or “us”) operates the website saltshore.net and distributes offline-first desktop software including CalGen, FinPro, and LedgerPro. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with this website and our products.

1. Offline-First Software

SaltShore Systems desktop applications (CalGen, FinPro, LedgerPro) are offline-first. They do not transmit your data to any remote server. All calendar files, invoice records, bank statement imports, and audit logs are stored locally on your own machine. SaltShore Systems has no access to any data you process with these tools.

2. Data We Collect via This Website

When you use the contact form on this website, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • The content of your message

This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry. We do not sell, rent, or share it with third parties.

3. Cookies & Tracking

This website does not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking scripts. Basic server access logs (IP address, request path, timestamp) may be retained by our hosting provider for security and diagnostic purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

We maintain a presence on Facebook. If you visit our Facebook page, Facebook's own privacy policy applies to data collected there. We do not embed Facebook tracking code on this website.

5. Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained only as long as necessary to address your inquiry. You may request deletion of any information you submitted by emailing support@saltshore.net.

6. Children’s Privacy

This website and our products are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision. Continued use of the website after a change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at support@saltshore.net.
